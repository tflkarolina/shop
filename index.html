Welcome to our magnets store!

Transform your fridge into a gallery of memories with our enchanting fridge magnets!

How does it work?

1. Upload Your Pictures

Upload your pictures from Camera Roll, edit and preview the pictures before submitting them. If you place an order, you will need to upload 9 pictures.

2. Place Order

When you are happy with your choice of pictures, simply add them to the cart, proceed to checkout, enter your shipping info, email and submit your order.

3. Track Your Order

Once the order is placed, we will print, cut, pack and ship your order. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking.

4. Enjoy your gallery of memories!
